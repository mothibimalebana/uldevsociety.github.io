Society Overview

The UL Developer Society is a community of developers. We're a community of diverse developers and techies dedicated to bridging the gap between university and work and providing our developers with skills that aren't taught in the classroom. We partner with companies and organizations to host events that help our members grow in software development. We have a place for anyone and everyone, no matter how much experience you have. ULDev welcomes students to the developer community and work environment.
